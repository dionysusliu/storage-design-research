<span id="about" class="anchor"></span> <span id="marquee" class="anchor"></span>

**SREcon26 Europe/Middle East/Africa** is a gathering of engineers who care deeply about site reliability, systems engineering, and working with complex distributed systems at scale. SREcon strives to challenge both those new to the profession as well as those who have been involved in it for decades. The conference has a culture of critical thought, deep technical insights, continuous improvement, and innovation.

Registration is <a href="https://www.usenix.org/conference/srecon26emea/registration-information" target="_blank">now open</a>, and the program will be available soon. The Early Bird Registration deadline is **Monday, 24 August, 2026**.

### Apply for a Grant

Learn more about <a href="https://www.usenix.org/conference/srecon26emea/grants" target="_blank">SREcon26 Europe/Middle East/Africa grant opportunities</a>. The application deadline is **Tuesday, 8 September, 2026**.
