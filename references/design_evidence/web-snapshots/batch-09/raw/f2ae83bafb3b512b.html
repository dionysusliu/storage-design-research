SREcon26 Europe/Middle East/Africa

 

 

DUBLIN, IRELAND
13–15 October, 2026

Grant applications are due 8 September, 2026

REGISTER NOW

SREcon26 Europe/Middle East/Africa is a gathering of engineers who care deeply about site reliability, systems engineering, and working with complex distributed systems at scale. SREcon strives to challenge both those new to the profession as well as those who have been involved in it for decades. The conference has a culture of critical thought, deep technical insights, continuous improvement, and innovation.

Registration is now open, and the program will be available soon. The Early Bird Registration deadline is Monday, 24 August, 2026.

Apply for a Grant

Learn more about SREcon26 Europe/Middle East/Africa grant opportunities. The application deadline is Tuesday, 8 September, 2026.

Registration Information

Early Bird Registration Deadline: Monday, 24 August, 2026

See the Registration Information page for details, including fees, discount information, and other deadlines.

Discounts

In addition to our member discounts, USENIX offers several discounts to help you to attend SREcon26 Europe/Middle East/Africa, including a hardship discount for USENIX members who are unemployed or retired, in between jobs, or lack employer support.

Accessiblity Information

USENIX is committed to ensuring that our meetings are fully accessible to all attendees. Visit our accessibility information page for details on mothers' rooms and how to make a request.

To ensure that we meet your needs, please make your request by Monday, 21 September, 2026. We cannot guarantee that we can meet requests received after this date, although we will make every reasonable effort to do so.
