# Core capabilities<a href="#core-capabilities" class="td-heading-self-link" aria-label="Heading self-link"></a>

Kafka boasts core capabilities that are battle tested and ready to power businesses in the digital world.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<span id="td-block-4" class="td-offset-anchor"></span>

<div class="mb-4 h1">

</div>

#### High Throughput

<div class="mb-0">

Deliver messages at network limited throughput using a cluster of machines with latencies as low as 2ms.

</div>

</div>

<div class="col-lg-4 mb-5 mb-lg-0 text-center">

<div class="mb-4 h1">

</div>

#### Scalable

<div class="mb-0">

Scale production clusters up to a thousand brokers, trillions of messages per day, petabytes of data, hundreds of thousands of partitions. Elastically expand and contract storage and processing.

</div>

</div>

<div class="col-lg-4 mb-5 mb-lg-0 text-center">

<div class="mb-4 h1">

</div>

#### Permanent Storage

<div class="mb-0">

Store streams of data safely in a distributed, durable, fault-tolerant cluster.

</div>

</div>

<div class="col-lg-4 mb-5 mb-lg-0 text-center">

<div class="mb-4 h1">

</div>

#### High Availability

<div class="mb-0">

Stretch clusters efficiently over availability zones or connect separate clusters across geographic regions.

</div>

</div>

<div class="col-lg-4 mb-5 mb-lg-0 text-center">

<div class="mb-4 h1">

</div>

#### Built-in Stream Processing

<div class="mb-0">

Process streams of events with joins, aggregations, filters, transformations, and more, using event-time and exactly-once processing.

</div>

</div>

<div class="col-lg-4 mb-5 mb-lg-0 text-center">

<div class="mb-4 h1">

</div>

#### Connect to almost anything

<div class="mb-0">

Kafka’s out-of-the-box Connect interface integrates with hundreds of event sources and event sinks including Postgres, JMS, Elasticsearch, AWS S3, and more.

</div>

</div>

</div>

</div>
