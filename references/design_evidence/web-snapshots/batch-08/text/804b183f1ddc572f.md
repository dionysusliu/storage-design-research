Thanks to those who joined us in Boston for the 19th USENIX Symposium on Operating Systems Design and Implementation (OSDI '25). We hope you enjoyed the event.

OSDI brings together professionals from academic and industrial backgrounds in what has become a premier forum for discussing the design, implementation, and implications of systems software. The symposium emphasizes innovative research as well as quantified or insightful experiences in systems design and implementation.

As part of our commitment to open access to research, the full proceedings and presentation slides are free and open to the public on the <a href="https://www.usenix.org/conference/osdi25/technical-sessions" style="font-weight: 600;">technical sessions</a> page. Videos are posted within a few weeks of the end of the event.

**Sheraton Boston Hotel**  
39 Dalton Street  
Boston, MA 02199  
+1 617.236.2000

Hotel Discount Deadline: ~~Monday, June 16~~ Monday, June 23—deadline extended!

USENIX has negotiated a special conference attendee room rate of US\$269 plus tax for single/double occupancy for conference attendees, including in-room wireless internet. To receive this rate, <a href="https://book.passkey.com/go/USENIX25ATC25" target="_blank">book your room online</a> or call the hotel and mention USENIX or OSDI '25 when making your reservation.

The group rate is available through **June 23,** or until the block sells out, whichever occurs first. After this date, contact the hotel directly to inquire about room availability.

## Room and Ride Sharing

USENIX maintains a <a href="https://groups.google.com/a/usenix.org/d/forum/roomshare" target="_blank">Google Group</a> to facilitate communication among attendees seeking roommates and ride sharing. You can sign up for free to find attendees with whom you can share a hotel room, taxi, shuttle, or other ride-share service. Please include **"OSDI '25"** in the subject line when posting a new request.
